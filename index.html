About Me

I was born from the Philippines and moved to the United States back in December 2014. After graduating High-School, I attended a semester at University of New Mexico studying Computer Engineering. Due to that degree, I was introduced on basics of Computer Programming. Unfortunately, I needed funding to pay for my college expenses, so I decided to join the Army. After Graduating Basic Combat Training and Advanced Individual Training, I was released to my designated unit at Belen, NM. After a couple of months I was then quickly deployed to help with the Covid Mission, and after that assignment I was deployed again to help with the Afghan refugee Camp at Virginia.

Following those deployments, I then continued to pursue my journey to start a career in Tech. I took a Bootcamp at CNM Ingenuity called Deep Dive Full Stack Web Development, there I learned everything in the stack and also gained experience what it's like working with a team of developers in a 40hr scheduled environment.
