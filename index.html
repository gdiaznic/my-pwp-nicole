About Me

I was born in the Philippines and moved to the United States back in December 2014. After graduating High-School, I attended a semester at the University of New Mexico studying Computer Engineering. Due to that degree, I was introduced to the basics of Computer Programming. Unfortunately, I needed funding to pay for my college expenses, so I decided to join the Army. After Graduating Basic Combat Training and Advanced Individual Training, I was released to my designated unit in Belen, NM.

After a couple of months I was then quickly deployed to help with the Covid Mission, and after that assignment I was deployed again to help with the Afghan refugee Camp in Virginia. Following those deployments, I then continued to pursue my journey to start a career in Tech. I took a Bootcamp at CNM Ingenuity called Deep Dive Full Stack Web Development, there I learned everything in the stack and also gained experience on what it's like working with a team of developers in a 40hrs scheduled environment.
